I have been fortunate enough in my short career to work with everything from old legacy server/client applications and modern event-driven applications. When I started my career in 2019 I thought I would become a backend developer but my interest in new tech and willingness to step up to a challenge, brought me into the world of Cloud-Native, DevOps and SRE. After having worked with DevOps for a while I got interested in the underlying infrastructure we built services on top of and this made me seek out new challenges in a startup, first as a Cloud and DevOps Engineer and then later as Cloud Architect and Tech.Lead. My experience has made me a supporter of cloud-native (albeit humbled), and I am a fierce opponent of complex tooling and complex documentation. My goal in my professional life is to make tools and systems that are easy to understand for developers and/or end-users.
